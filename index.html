Tara (Tayebeh) Saleh

I am Tayebeh, a Senior research scientist in the field of nanobiotechnology. Briefly, my research is more focused on designing different nanoparticles as drug delivery systems. This topic was in line with my previous research experience during my Ph.D. when I extensively worked on the approaches for enhancing the potency of DNA vaccine.
